MYSURU
Rich tributes were paid to Mahatma Gandhi on the occasion of his 154th birth anniversary in Mysuru on Monday.
The city also remembered former Prime Minister Lal Bahadur Shastri on his birth anniversary.
Gandhi Jayanti was celebrated at Town Hall with programs organized by the district administration, Mysuru City Corporation, and the Department of Information and Public Relations.
